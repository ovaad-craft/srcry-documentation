Crush Gap

There may be times where your app or website will be used on screens that are smaller than the baseSize of your element. In order to prevent issues with overflow you can use the crushGap properties to make your elements scale down accordingly. Simply put, the crushGap is the amount of margin to maintain between the element and the edges of the screen. The chart below outlines the usage of these properties.
